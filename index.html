CrossFit

A form of high intensity interval training, CrossFit is a strength and conditioning workout that is made up of functional movement performed at a high intensity level.

Weight-Traning

Increased muscle mass: Muscle mass naturally decreases with age, but strength training can help reverse the trend. Stronger bones: Strength training increases bone density and reduces the risk of fractures.

Boxercise

What do you do in boxercise? Boxercise is an exercise class based on the training concepts boxers use to keep fit. Classes can take a variety of formats but a typical one may involve shadow-boxing, skipping, hitting pads, kicking punchbags, press-ups, shuttle-runs and sit-ups.
